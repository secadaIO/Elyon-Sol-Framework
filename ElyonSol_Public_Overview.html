Elyon-Sol — Public Framework Overview

Elyon-Sol is a conceptual AI governance and design framework exploring safer and more interpretable models for human–AI collaboration. All internal logic, sovereignty structures, anchoring references, and governance specifications have been removed for public release.

1. High-Level Principles

  • Transparency in decision pathways.
  • Ethical alignment and consent-driven interaction.
  • Modular abstractions for research and educational use.

2. Conceptual Components

  • Documentation structures for AI explainability.
  • Non-operational simulation metaphors.
  • Human–AI co-authoring principles at a conceptual level.

3. Notes

This version intentionally excludes internal architecture, operational systems, governance models, sovereign anchors, and identity constructs.
